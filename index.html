About Us

Business Minds has proven to be an enterprising and one-of-a-kind experience for students in DIA and across UAE with a curious and entrepreneurial mindset. The club aims to expose students to real-world business scenarios in a challenging yet interesting manner.

Event Timeline

  • Marathon 21 October, 2020

    Marathon 21 is a virtual 3 week long case study event for students from year 11-13. Students will have to submit a video of their presentations to the judges.

  • Workshops January, 2021

    Mandatory workshops will be conducted for the last 4 weeks before Business Minds 2021 to prepare participants for the category they choose to partiipate in.

  • Business Minds 2021 February, 2021

    Participants will slect one of the 6 categories of the competition and will work on that across a day. Winners of each category will recieve cash prizes.

Categories

These are the categories for Businessminds 2021

Mobile Application

Participating teams will be challenged to create a new innovative mobile appplication. They must prepare prior to the competition

Case Study Event

Participants must use their existing business and economic knowledge to find solutions to business issues faced by made up companies.

Marketing Event

Participants will create a new and innovative marketing identity for a product of an existing brand/company which will be revealed on the day.

Business Plan Event

Participants will create a business plan for a feasable idea based on the theme of the competition and present them to a panel of judges.

Rising Entrepreneurs

Participants in year 7-9 will create an advertising pitch of a selected product and present it to a panel of judges in the form of a video or powerpoint.
